Yuria the witch

Yuria is a witch that practices a dark form of Soul Arts. Yuria wears the hat of a witch, but the insect-infested clothing of one who has lived through horrific things

The Maiden in Black

The Maiden in Black, who aids the demon slayers with her command of Soul power, was once one of the oldest, most dangerous demons herself. The Black-Robed Maiden is a morose type, with eyes occluded by wax. She can control souls like no other.

Yurt, the Silent Chief

Yurt, the Silent Chief, is a deadly assassin at the service of a secret masked society that seeks to harness the power of souls. He entered Boletaria with the simple task of killing anyone knowledgeable in Soul Arts, but ended up imprisoned in Latria.

Penetrator

Penetrator is the third boss in the Boletarian Palace Archstone, found at the end of the Inner Ward zone. He is a powerful knight wielding a spear-like longsword imbued with magic energy.
